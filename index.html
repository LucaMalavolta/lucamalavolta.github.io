I’m an observational astronomer focused on the detection and characterization planetary systems orbiting nearby stars. I use photometry from space and high-resolution spectropy from the ground to measure masses and radii of exoplanets, and constrain their bulk compositions. I am curious about how planets form and evolve with time, the frequency of planetary systems in young Open Clusters and Associations, and the prospects for detecting an Earth analogue close to our Solar System. There's a good chance you'll meet me at some telescope at the end of an observing run!

I have a fixed-term staff researcher postion at INAF-Observatory of Catania
